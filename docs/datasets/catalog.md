# Datasets Catalog

Drawline Core v0.2.0 features **60+ semantic datasets**. Below is a categorized list of the primary domains supported out-of-the-box.

## 💳 Finance & Commerce
* `financial_banks_india.json`: Major Indian banks.
* `financial_tax_types.json`: GST, VAT, Sales Tax, etc.
* `finance_investment_types.json`: Stocks, Bonds, Mutual Funds.
* `commerce_payment_methods.json`: Credit Card, PayPal, UPI.
* `commerce_order_statuses.json`: Shipped, Pending, Cancelled.

## 🏥 Healthcare & Life Sciences
* `healthcare_specialties.json`: Cardiology, Neurology, etc.
* `healthcare_vital_types.json`: BP, Heart Rate, SpO2.
* `anatomy_organs.json`: Lungs, Heart, Brain.
* `science_elements.json`: Periodic table elements.
* `science_planets.json`: Solar system bodies.

## ✈️ Aviation & Logistics
* `aviation_airlines.json`: Global airlines.
* `aviation_flight_statuses.json`: On-time, Delayed, Landed.
* `logistics_carriers.json`: FedEx, DHL, BlueDart.
* `logistics_units.json`: kg, lbs, cubic meters.

## 💻 Technology & Infrastructure
* `tech_programming_languages.json`: TS, Go, Rust, Python.
* `tech_databases.json`: PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Redis.
* `tech_cloud_providers.json`: AWS, GCP, Azure.
* `tech_protocols.json`: gRPC, MQTT, HTTPS.
* `media_resolutions.json`: 4K, 1080p, 720p.

## ⚖️ Legal & Government
* `legal_case_statuses.json`: Open, Dismissed, Appealed.
* `legal_document_types.json`: NDA, MSA, Privacy Policy.
* `government_id_types_india.json`: Aadhaar, PAN, Voter ID.
* `geography_iso_codes.json`: Full ISO-3166-1 alpha-3 codes.

## 👤 People & Places
* `names_indian.json`: Culturally accurate Indian names.
* `names_global.json`: Diverse international names.
* `geography_countries.json`: List of all global nations.
* `geography_states_india.json`: All Indian states and UTs.
* `job_titles.json`: Extensive list of 500+ professional roles.
